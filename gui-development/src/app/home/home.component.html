What do we provide?

Our application provides a general overview in terms of finance and finance planning.

We provide:
  • Monthly statement of movements in your bank account.
  • An overview of all transactions for a specific date.
  • Detailed planning of future investments.

The application is connected to the banking system from which the data for payments. These data are displayed in graphs and in the tables when filtering for a specific date. With this data it is possible further create a plan in which you get a detailed overview of your income, including planned investments. The output of planning gives you a month or year when you can make a planned investment.

How does it work?
Registration

To access the functions of our portal, you need to create a free account. Can be used for registration menu, in which the link to the registration is located. After successfully filling in the necessary data for registration, you will use in menu link to log in, then a window will appear in which you will fill in your data.

Login

To log in, click on the Login item in the menu. Enter your email and password chosen during registration.

Edit Profile

After successful login, an icon will appear in the upper right corner symbolizing your profile, in this section you you can edit or complete the necessary data. Further in this section you will find the password change and the option to delete the account.

My Overview

The logged-in user gains access to the My Overview section via the menu.

This section is divided into 2 sections:
  • Savings Summary
  • Transaction Summary
Savings Overview

To access this section, you must fill in your account number. Once you do, the application calculates Your monthly movement in the bank account will evaluate the net monthly profit and display the entire monthly movement in a graph.
The displayed data are always calculated and displayed for the previous month. However, if you do not have it set transaction, don't despair, you can change your monthly earnings using the box below the monthly movement chart (for display use this icon).

Your monthly earnings must be positive, non-zero. After possible adjustment of the income, there is a box for setting yours possible amounts saved. This amount can be zero.

Once you have set the mentioned data, 4 graphs will be displayed. Everyone can choose the type of chart that prefers from the menu is here bar and linear graph. The graphs show the future status of your account. This status is displayed with a certain tolerances. There is a choice immediately 5 possible (-5%, -2.5%, 0%, 2.5%, 5%). These tolerances are used to display possible fluctuations of yours net income.

This tolerance can be filtered in the graph by clicking on the selected legend (at the top of the graph).

So, for example, -5% represents that your monthly income will decrease by 5%, while 2.5% represents that your monthly income will increase by 2.5%.

Another option is to use a wish sheet. This sheet is used to plan your future investment. If this you insert the wish into the sheet, the graphs will automatically change according to your input, the result will be the display in the graph, when you can make the given wish to meet.
